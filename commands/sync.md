# /sync — Sync Entries to Google Sheet

Push your confirmed time entries to the Google Sheet. Entries are appended to the Daily Time Log tab and the Daily Dashboard is updated.

## Usage

```
/sync
```

Run after `/log` to push your reviewed entries. You can also say "sync" during a log session after confirming the table.

## What Happens

1. Confirmed entries are written to the Daily Time Log tab
2. Daily Dashboard row is updated with totals
3. Charts & Dashboards data tables are refreshed
4. You see a confirmation with row count and revenue breakdown

# /log — Dictate Your Day

Dictate what you did today in natural language. The tracker will parse your words into structured time entries with categories, projects, energy levels, and revenue tags.

## Usage

```
/log
I woke up at 9:45. Made coffee and checked email until 10:15.
From 10:15 to 10:45 I played with the kids outside.
Then worked on the daily tracker until 11:45.
Had lunch around noon for 30 minutes.
CC session on amt-aiops from 12:30 to 3 PM.
```

## What Happens

1. Your dictation is parsed into a structured table
2. Times, categories, projects, and energy levels are auto-assigned
3. You review the table and make any edits
4. Say "sync" to push confirmed entries to your Google Sheet

# /review — Weekly Review

Generate a full weekly review with time allocation charts, revenue focus breakdown, project rankings, habit scores, and reflection prompts.

## Usage

```
/review
/review last week
/review week of April 7
```

## What Happens

1. Reads the past 7 days from your Google Sheet
2. Shows targets vs actuals for each category
3. Breaks down revenue focus (Earning/Building/Seeding/Internal)
4. Lists top 5 projects by hours
5. Shows habit completion scores
6. Presents reflection prompts for you to fill in
7. Saves the review to the Weekly Summary tab
